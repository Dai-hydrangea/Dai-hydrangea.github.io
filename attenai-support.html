About Attenai

Attenai is a philosophical conversation app where Socrates, Nietzsche, and Jung discuss your everyday questions — seriously. From "Which is better: chocolate or vanilla?" to "What is freedom?", these great thinkers will philosophize about anything.

Features

  • 🏛️ Three philosophers respond to any question
  • 🔄 "One more round" — watch them debate each other
  • 💡 Tap "Glossary" for easy explanations of philosophical terms
  • 📚 Book & movie recommendations from the philosophers
  • 🧒👤🎓 Three difficulty levels: Kids, Normal, Scholar
  • 🌐 Japanese, English, and Chinese support

FAQ

What kind of questions can I ask?

Anything! The sillier, the better. "Is curry spicy?" or "Dogs vs cats?" — the philosophers will take it seriously.

Is the information accurate?

Responses are AI-generated and meant for entertainment and learning. Always verify important information from original sources.

What does "Attenai" mean?

It's a play on "Athenai" (ancient Athens, birthplace of philosophy) and the Japanese phrase "atteinai" (合ってない = "not quite right"). Because the best conversations start when the answers don't quite match.
