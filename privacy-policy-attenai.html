Attenai Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Attenai is a philosophical conversation app where great thinkers discuss your everyday questions. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and is NOT sent to any external server:

  • Conversation history and keywords
  • App settings and preferences
  • Photos taken for image recognition

3. External Services

Attenai communicates with the following external service:

  • Google Gemini API: Used for generating philosopher responses, term explanations, and book recommendations. Your questions and conversation context are sent to the API for processing.

Data sent to external services is limited to the minimum required for functionality.

4. AI-Generated Content

All philosopher responses and explanations are generated by artificial intelligence and may not be fully accurate. They are intended for entertainment and educational purposes. Please verify important information from original sources.

5. Data Storage

All user data is stored only on your device. There is no cloud backup or synchronization. Deleting the app will permanently remove all data.

6. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to Amazon for book recommendations. These links open in your external browser. We do not collect any data about your purchases.

7. Children's Privacy

Attenai includes a "Kids Mode" for younger users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: daihydrangea.apps@gmail.com
